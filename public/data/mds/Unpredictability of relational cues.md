## Disruption and unpredictability of relational cues

One of the key references for the visual identity is a weather glyph used by a meteorologist at a weather station in Ferizaj, Kosovo, to mark phenomena that can only be observed, not measured. This symbol becomes part of the language of the project, highlighting what escapes standard forms of representation.

The visual identity of the Pavilion of the Republic of Kosovo 2025 reflects and plays with the disruption and unpredictability of relational cues, creating its own code-language that disrupts the known alphabet. It challenges familiar systems of communication, embracing ambiguity, complexity, and transformation.
